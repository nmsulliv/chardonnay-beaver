Multimedia Storytelling

I believe that storytelling and civic engagement invokes connection and empathy, promoting measurable progress and social good. I've spent the entirety of my professional life amplifying the stories of minoritized groups.

Public Speaking

I began public speaking at community gatherings at the age of 12. Since then, I've hosted panels, facilitated interviews, engaged audiences as a keynote speaker, and more. Some of those platforms include, but aren't limited to: Museum of History and Industry (MOHAI), Converge Media, YWCA Women of Achievement Awards, Blacks at Microsoft, and Justice for Girls.
