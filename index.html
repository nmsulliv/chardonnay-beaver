Who is Chardonnay Beaver?

I am Chardonnay Beaver, a multimedia storyteller, influential speaker, and journalist from Seattle. I am a proud University of Washington alumni, earning her B.A. in political science and journalism & public interest communication; coupled with a diversity studies minor. I am a third generation media professional. Some of my favorite things to do involve anywhere with an ocean view, delicious food, family, and live music.

What is my vision?

My vision is to use storytelling as a means to empower individuals to live authentic, purpose-driven that inform, equip, and engage collectives within spheres of influence. These spheres includes, but aren't limited to: family, religion, education, media, entertainment, business, and government.
